Something About Our Roots

Hiwet Bakery, founded by Abraham after his move from Asmara to Addis Ababa, has become a staple in the city's vibrant food scene. Inspired by a deep passion for baking and a desire to share his rich cultural heritage, Abraham started the bakery with the goal of offering unique, high-quality baked goods that stand out from the crowd. Over the years, Hiwet Bakery has earned a reputation for its exceptional flavors and attention to detail, with each product made using the finest ingredients and crafted with care. What makes Hiwet Bakery truly special is not just the delicious treats it offers, but also its connection to the community. Locals and visitors alike eagerly line up, especially during the holidays, to take home the bakery's renowned offerings. From soft breads and pastries to delightful sweets, Hiwet Bakery provides a warm and inviting experience that brings people together. Abraham’s commitment to excellence, combined with the bakery's welcoming atmosphere, has made it a beloved spot for anyone seeking authentic and freshly made baked goods. Whether it's for a special occasion or just a daily indulgence, Hiwet Bakery continues to delight its customers and remains a cherished part of Addis Ababa's culinary landscape.
